Athekkad

About Athekkad

Athekkad is a locality in the Thrissur district of Kerala, India. It is known for its peaceful surroundings, green landscapes, and friendly community. The area is primarily residential and is well connected by road to nearby towns and to Thrissur city.

Nature

The locality is surrounded by coconut trees, paddy fields, and other tropical vegetation. Agriculture has traditionally been an important part of life in the area.

Culture

People of Athekkad celebrate festivals such as Onam, Vishu, Christmas, and Eid with great enthusiasm, reflecting the rich cultural diversity of Kerala.

Lifestyle

Athekkad offers a calm and pleasant environment. The people are known for their hospitality, and the area has access to schools, places of worship, shops, and other essential facilities.
